I am a neuroscience researcher developing flexible neural interfaces for stable electrophysiology and optogenetics in nonhuman primates.

My work combines microfabrication, implantation strategy, and systems neuroscience, with the goal of building better tools for studying how cortical circuits regulate physiological state.

I am currently a third-year PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at Vanderbilt University and a member of the Gonzales Lab.
