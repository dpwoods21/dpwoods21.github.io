Biomedical Engineering PhD student at Vanderbilt University

I study how flexible electronics can be used to improve neural interfaces. I develop and apply these interfaces to improve applications of recordings in large animal models, for use in chronic settings, and for application with optogenetics.

My work combines systems microfabrication, electrophysiology, and behavioral neuroscience.
